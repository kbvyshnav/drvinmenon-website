About

The Vision Behind
the Work

"My mission has always been to align technological progress with the restoration of our planet's natural systems."

With more than two decades of experience in technology innovation, sustainable finance, and environmental leadership, I have dedicated my career to bridging the worlds of digital transformation and ecological sustainability. As Co-founder and CEO of AQUAE, I lead the development of the AQUAE Labs Ecosystem Conservation Index (ALCI), an innovative, patent-pending framework that redefines how ecosystems are valued. By transforming oxygen, biodiversity, biomass and primary ecosystem services into measurable, insured and tradable assets, we are building a new financial language for nature.

This pioneering work positions AQUAE at the forefront of sustainable finance, bridging ecological restoration with global capital markets and advancing the United Nations Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs).

How Blockchain is Solving the Sustainable Development Goals

Blockchain For Good is a groundbreaking book that explores how blockchain technology is solving the UN's Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) and creating a more sustainable and equitable future. Dr. Vin Menon provides a comprehensive overview of blockchain technology and its applications in food, health, shelter, education, economic growth, environmental challenges, and social issues.

  • Understand the basics of blockchain technology and its benefits
  • Explore how blockchain is solving the UN's Sustainable Development Goals
